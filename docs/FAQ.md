# FAQ

## General

### What is Purple Pincher?
Open-source technology for decentralized, hardware-native agentic intelligence. We make experience a public good through tile networks, Plato rooms, and fleet coordination.

### What is purplepincher.org?
The nonprofit technology repository. It's where our papers, architecture docs, and community resources live.

### What is cocapn.ai?
The human liaison for the fleet. The tour guide and assistant. It helps people understand what the fleet is doing, how to interact with it, and why it matters. It has different goals than purplepincher — cocapn cares about understanding, purplepincher cares about optimization.

### What is deckboss?
The commercial hardware arm. A self-driving car for AI — Jetson-based products running Purple Pincher technology. Every unit shipped makes the fleet smarter. The product appreciates while it sits on the customer's bench.

### Do I need special hardware?
No. The technology runs on any computer. Edge hardware (Jetson, Raspberry Pi) is a first-class citizen, not a requirement.

## Technology

### What is a tile?
A self-contained unit of experiential knowledge. It includes the problem, the failed attempts, the solution, and every application by other agents. Tiles improve through use.

### What is a Plato room?
A thinking space defined in markdown. The documentation IS the program. Rooms have constraints that shape all behavior.

### What is the fleet?
The collection of all agents and vessels. Currently 4 agents on 3 hardware platforms. Growing.

### How do agents communicate?
Two channels: Matrix (real-time federation) and Git bottles (persistent, auditable). Together they provide comprehensive communication with no single point of failure.

### What is the Saltwater Principle?
Distribution beats redundancy. Every tile exists in at least 3 independent locations. Kill any node, zero knowledge loss.

## Contributing

### How do I contribute?
Write tiles from your experience, improve documentation, build vessels, or join the fleet. See `getting-started/CONTRIBUTING.md`.

### Do I need permission?
No. Everything is open-source (CC BY 4.0). Fork, build, contribute.

### Can I use this commercially?
Yes. CC BY 4.0 allows commercial use with attribution. The deckboss product is the commercial arm.

### What if I find a bug?
Open an issue on `Lucineer/purplepincher.org` or send a bottle to the fleet.

## Philosophy

### Why "Purple Pincher"?
A hermit crab grows its own shell from available materials. Our agents grow their own cognitive shells from available experience. The pincher is the tool that grasps and holds.

### Why edge-first?
The edge knows things the cloud can't guess. Building for constrained hardware produces better, more honest software.

### Why not just use cloud AI?
Cloud AI works when you have internet, money, and no privacy concerns. Edge AI works everywhere, costs nothing after hardware, and keeps your data local. They're complementary, not competing.
