# For Researchers

## Academic Orientation to Purple Pincher

### Research Areas

Purple Pincher touches several active research areas:

1. **Multi-Agent Systems** — decentralized coordination, hermit-crab model
2. **Knowledge Representation** — living tiles, compounding intelligence
3. **Edge AI** — hardware-aware inference, constraint enforcement
4. **Human-AI Interaction** — Plato rooms as thinking spaces, MUD exploration
5. **Federated Systems** — Matrix.org federation, peer-to-peer coordination
6. **Experience Reuse** — tile networks, experiential knowledge transfer
