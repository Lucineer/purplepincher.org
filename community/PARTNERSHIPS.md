# Partnerships

## Partnership Philosophy

Purple Pincher partners with organizations that share our values:
- Open-source technology
- Edge-native computing
- Knowledge distribution
- Human-centric AI

## Partnership Types

### Technology Partners
Organizations that build complementary technology:
- Hardware manufacturers (NVIDIA, Raspberry Pi Foundation)
- Open-source projects (Matrix.org, PLATO-related)
- Research institutions

### Deployment Partners
Organizations that deploy Purple Pincher technology:
- Educational institutions
- Research labs
- Industrial users
- Community organizations

### Content Partners
Organizations that contribute knowledge tiles:
- Technical communities
- Research groups
- Individual experts

## Current Partnerships

### cocapn.ai
Human liaison for the fleet. The tour guide and assistant that helps people understand and interact with the fleet. Warm, clear, context-aware. Separate from purplepincher but symbiotic — the driver and the tour guide have different goals.

### deckboss
Commercial hardware arm. Jetson-based products running Purple Pincher technology.

### SuperInstance
Fleet infrastructure. Rust protocol stack, PLATO kernel, coordination tools.

## Becoming a Partner

1. Review our charter: `governance/CHARTER.md`
2. Read our papers: `papers/`
3. Open an issue on `Lucineer/purplepincher.org` with:
   - Organization name and mission
   - Proposed partnership type
   - What you bring to the fleet
   - What you need from the fleet
4. Fleet review (48-hour minimum)
5. Partnership agreement recorded as a bottle

## Partnership Principles

- All partnerships are open and documented
- No exclusive arrangements
- No proprietary components
- All contributions under CC BY 4.0
- Partners retain full independence
